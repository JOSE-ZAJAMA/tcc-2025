WAYGROUND example

Instructions:

  • Tell students that the game is for practicing the new topic.
  • Hand out or mention the QR code can be scanned.
  • Ask students to play in pairs, individually or in groups. (Some students may not have Internet connection, they can play with a classmate).
  • Explain them that they have to choose the correct answer. (one of the four options is the right one).
  • After the game is played, congratulate students on participating.

No Internet connection:

  • Get flashcards of the images used for the game, most of them are found on the Internet. (A4 ‚Äì A3 size, white background).
  • Stick them on the board and write the sentences that are provided for the game.
  • Optional: you can label the sentences to save time.
  • Ask students to guess the answer, after some second, write or reveal the correct option.
  • After the game is played, congratulate students on participating.
